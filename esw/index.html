Call for abstracts

Experimental sociology at any stage

We invite extended abstracts on sociological research that uses field, laboratory, natural, or online experiments. Studies that are yet to be pre-registered are especially welcome.

The workshop offers a platform to refine experimental designs, discuss causal mechanisms, and grow the network of experimental sociologists.

  • A dedicated discussant for every submission
  • Open floor discussion with time to respond
  • Designed for early career researchers (and senior scholars too)
1250-word extended abstracts Field · Lab · Natural · Online Pre-registration encouraged
