Global collaboration for experimental sociology

Accelerating reliable, rigorous, and reproducible evidence

The Sociological Science Accelerator (SSA) pools expertise, funding, and infrastructure to coordinate studies that are democratically selected and openly run by its members, managing registered reports, pre-registrations, study designs, data collection, analysis, and dissemination for sociological studies.
